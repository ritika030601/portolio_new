ABOUT ME

My journey started in the terminal, shipping app features as a software engineer.

The Engineering Foundation

My career began in Pune in January 2023 as a software engineering intern at Principal Global Services. What started as an internship quickly transformed into a full-time role, where I spent over two years building and scaling mobile applications alongside a talented engineering team. I wasn't just writing code—I was solving complex problems that directly impacted thousands of users. Every feature I built, every component I refactored, and every production deployment taught me something profound about how systems work at scale. That hands-on experience with building real products from the ground up became the foundation for everything I do as a designer today.

The Shift: From Code to Clarity

While I genuinely loved the problem-solving aspect of software engineering, something unexpected happened. I found myself paying more attention to the design of the interfaces we were building than the code underneath. I realized I was constantly asking: "Why does this flow feel awkward?" and "How can we make this clearer for users?" rather than focusing purely on technical implementation. This shift in perspective revealed my true passion—I wanted to be the person designing the solution itself, not just implementing someone else's design decisions.

To transform this passion into a concrete skill set, I enrolled in an intensive UI/UX design diploma program from November 2025 to April 2026. It was a transformative journey that covered everything from fundamental design principles and user research methodologies to advanced concepts in design systems and prototyping. I didn't just learn theory—I applied every concept to real-world projects, creating comprehensive case studies that demonstrated research-driven design thinking.

The Advantage: An Engineer's Mind for Design

Today, my approach to design is genuinely unique because I bring an engineer's perspective to every project. I don't design beautiful screens that can't be built—I design with a deep understanding of how systems actually work, how data flows through components, and how designs scale across different devices and contexts. Because I've lived on both sides, I can speak the language of developers and bridge the gap between design intent and technical reality. This background means I create experiences that are not only thoughtfully designed but also practically implementable, making collaboration with development teams smooth and efficient. I'm passionate about building user-centered products where every interaction is intentional and every design decision serves a purpose.
