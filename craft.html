ABOUT ME

My journey started in the terminal, shipping app features as a software engineer.

Coding the Foundation

In January 2023, I packed my bags and moved to Pune to start a new chapter as a software engineering intern. That internship turned into a full-time role, and over the years, I grew into a full-time software engineer. I spent my days deep in the code—building, updating, and scaling mobile applications. From handling complex user features to working on major project rollovers like the builders' investment pages, I learned exactly what it takes to bring a complex product to life from scratch.

Finding the "Why" Behind the Code

While I loved solving technical puzzles, working on those mobile apps made me realize something important: I was constantly looking past the code and focusing on the experience. I found myself deeply invested in how the user interacted with what we built. I wanted to be the one designing the solution, not just writing the logic for it.

To turn that passion into a reality, I jumped into an intensive UI/UX diploma program from November 2025 to April 2026. It was an incredible journey where I mastered everything from core design laws to user research, turning those concepts into real, hands-on Figma projects and comprehensive case studies.

Designing with a Developer’s Mind

Today, I look at design a little differently. Because of my engineering background, I don’t just design pretty screens—I design with a deep understanding of how data flows and how components scale. I know exactly what’s happening under the hood, which makes it easy for me to collaborate smoothly with development teams and build user-friendly experiences that actually solve real-world problems.
