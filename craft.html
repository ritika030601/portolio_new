About Me

My journey started in the terminal, shipping app features as a software engineer.

I realized that great products need more than clean code. Users need clear, intuitive experiences that make them feel understood. That insight is what moved me from app engineering into UI/UX design.

Today I use my engineering rigor to inform every design decision. I understand how data flows, how components scale at the system level, and how to communicate precisely with developers to ensure the final product looks and feels exactly as intended. I don't just design for the screen — I design for the system.

I do not stop at handoff. I stay involved through PR review to ensure implementation matches design intent.
