ABOUT ME

My journey started in the terminal, shipping app features as a software engineer.

The Engineering Foundation

My career began in Pune in January 2023, as a software engineering intern at Principal Global Services. The internship turned into a full-time role, and over the next two years I helped build and scale mobile applications with a great engineering team. I wasn't just writing code - I was solving problems that affected thousands of users, and that hands-on experience with real products is still the foundation for how I think about design today.

The Shift: From Code to Clarity

I loved the problem-solving side of engineering, but over time I noticed I was paying more attention to how the interfaces looked and felt than to the code behind them. I kept asking myself why a flow felt awkward, or how it could be clearer for users - and that's when I realised I wanted to be the person designing the solution, not just building it.

To turn that into an actual skill set, I joined a UI/UX design diploma program from November 2025 to April 2026, covering everything from design fundamentals and user research to design systems and prototyping. I made a point of applying what I learned to real projects, turning each one into a case study grounded in research rather than guesswork.

The Advantage: An Engineer's Mind for Design

Having worked on both sides means I bring an engineer's perspective to design. I don't design screens that look good but are hard to build - I think about how data moves through a system and how a design holds up across devices. That makes it easier to work with development teams, since I already speak their language. What matters most to me is building products where every interaction has a clear reason behind it.
