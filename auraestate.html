Case Study - Dual-Mode Real Estate

04. Aura Estate

Aura Estate is a premium platform built to serve two distinct high-intent personas through a shared entry point that quickly splits into investor and buyer experiences.

View full case study on Behance

The Problem

Investors and buyers were forced into the same generic flow, creating confusion and undermining confidence for both audiences.

Investor and buyer needs were combined into one noisy journey.
High-intent actions were not clearly surfaced for either persona.
The interface lacked personalised decision support and trust cues.

The Solution

Create a single shared entry point with a persona toggle.
Build separate investor and buyer flows with tailored dashboards.
Keep the brand consistent while making each mode feel custom.

Research Focus

Research centred on dual persona interviews and comparative journey mapping to confirm the need for divergent experiences.

Interviewed investors and buyers to capture distinct goals.
Mapped decision milestones and trust expectations for each persona.
Validated the importance of a quick choice between modes.
