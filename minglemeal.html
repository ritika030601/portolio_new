Case Study - Food Rescue Network

03. MingleMeal

MingleMeal connects restaurants, volunteers, and NGOs to simplify food rescue logistics and reduce waste through a role-based product flow.

View full case study on Behance

The Problem

Coordination gaps and unclear handoffs between donors, volunteers, and receivers made food rescue slow and unreliable.

Donations stalled without an accountable, end-to-end flow.
Volunteers lacked clear pickup direction and verification.
NGOs needed faster visibility into arriving food and status updates.

The Solution

Design role-specific flows for donors, volunteers, and NGOs.
Use status updates and QR verification to improve reliability.
Provide live pickup tracking and simple confirmation for every handoff.

Research Focus

Research focused on end-to-end logistics, trust signals, and the needs of every stakeholder in the rescue chain.

Interviewed donors, drivers, and NGOs to understand friction points.
Mapped handoff states from donation to pickup to delivery.
Validated that clear verification builds confidence and reduces errors.
